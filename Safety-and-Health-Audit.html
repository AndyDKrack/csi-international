Safety & Health Audit

At CSI International Limited, we conduct comprehensive Safety and Health Audits to help organizations foster safe, compliant, and responsible work environments. We understand the importance of protecting employees’ health, preserving the environment, and adhering to legal and regulatory standards. With a growing demand for clear evidence of adherence to industry best practices. Our audits provide a systematic evaluation of workplace health and safety programs, aiming to uncover potential risks, ensure regulatory compliance, and support continuous improvement in occupational health and environmental performance.

Key audit types include but not limited to:
  • Compliance Audits – Verify adherence to regulations (OSHA, ISO 45001, EPA).
  • Management System Audits – Assess the effectiveness of OHS frameworks.
  • Risk-Based Audits – Identify hazards and prioritize high-risk areas.
  • Process Safety Audits – Prevent incidents in industries handling hazardous materials.
  • Workplace Safety Audits – Evaluate fire safety, PPE usage, and emergency preparedness.
  • Behavioral Safety Audits – Monitor employee safety practices and culture.
  • Contractor Safety Audits – Ensure third-party compliance with safety standards.
  • EHS Audits – Assess both workplace safety and environmental impact.
  • Fire Safety Audits – Inspect fire prevention, alarms, and evacuation plans.
  • Ergonomic Audits – Improve workplace ergonomics to prevent musculoskeletal issues.
