Pre-Employment Screening

Security Vetting, Background checks and Personnel Screening

Research has indicated the level of fraud and theft related cases perpetrated by employees are on the increase. Another major concern is the escalating cases of collusion between the employees and the third parties engaged in business with the company. To proactively minimize human risk, CSI International Ltd will develop several techniques to keep undesirable people out of the company and foster the development of an honest workforce. An approach will be developed to thoroughly screen your employees working in critical assignments. Background checks provide a company with valuable information about individuals and businesses - about your associates, acquaintances, competitors, and potential employees. CSI International Ltd assists employers in obtaining background information on prospective employees to aid in the hiring process.
