Our Services

Research and Development Quality Assurance.

Since the inception of CSI International, we strive to consistently offer efficient, reliable and customized services to our clients. We continue to improve and expand our services to meet client needs through customer feedback.

All our staff are professionally trained and have a wealth of experience and knowledge in Research and Development including: sampling, testing, data analysis and acquisition to provide accurate and timely results.

Our staff comprises of members with qualifications ranging from HND, BSc., MSc. and PhD in Physical Chemistry, Biological, Environmental and Agricultural Sciences.

Stack Emission Analysis

The flue gas parameters are guided by KS 1515 and National Environment and Coordination Act of 1999. We check for compliance with the maximum standard limits and provide recommendations. We submit report for EDL licensing by NEMA.

Effluent Discharge Sampling

The Water effluent parameters are guided by National Environment and Coordination Act (Water Quality Guidelines) of 2006 as per NEMA guidelines. We check effluent discharge against maximum standard limits for discharge into public sewers or into the environment.

Interlab Services

CSI International provides accredited Interlab Services, offering a range of testing and analysis solutions. We specialize in environmental testing, including water quality, soil analysis, air quality, and more. With advanced equipment and expert technicians, we deliver accurate, reliable results to ensure compliance and safety for various industries.

Air Quality Monitoring

CSI International Limited is NEMA designated and accredited by KENAS to carry out air quality monitoring according to local and international standards. Emissions from Boilers, generators, Incinerators, Burners, Asphalt Plants, Sulphonation Plants, and several other industrial processes emit pollutants that are either gaseous or particulates into the environment.

Laboratory Services

The laboratory is NEMA designated and accredited by KENAS for Laboratory Testing (ISO/IEC 17025:2017), fulfilling all the requirements. As an analytical laboratory, we are committed to providing affordable and the highest quality in all services rendered with the lowest turn-around time. We offer a range of laboratory services (Physical, Chemical, and Microbial).

Health, Safety, and Environmental Management Services

With the ever-increasing environmental management standards, compliance, and stringent environmental regulations, CSI International Limited has stationed itself as a DOSHS and NEMA registered Consultancy Company with registered experts to operate a one-stop network where all Environment, Safety and Health compliance.

Soil Sampling and Analysis

We offer chemical and mechanical analysis of soils for variety of uses. Our mission is to provide accurate and timely soil test reports to meet the unique agricultural and environmental needs of our country. We provide information on techniques of soil sampling and carry out various analyses on agricultural soil.

Water Analysis

Our staff performs water testing for inorganic and volatile organic compounds for drinking, domestic and waste water. We have instrumentation dedicated to water testing and test for all the regulated and non-regulated compounds of concern. Our lab also carry out a wider range of analyses relevant to the health related parameters laid down by WHO water quality standards.

Agrochemicals/Pesticide Residue Analysis

Agricultural activities such as Horticulture and Floriculture rely heavily on use of farm inputs such as agrochemicals, pesticides and fertilizers. In pesticide analysis we determine levels of remnant residues of pesticides in plants, food and soils. This in important for the consumers to be assured of the quality and level of residues within food.

Research & Data Analysis

We offer unrivalled and professional research testing services and data analysis for companies, institutions and students undertaking research. Be it for your graduate or post graduate degree, we have you covered.

Environmental Impact Assessment and Audit

We perform Environmental Impact Assessment to identify and predict the impact of a project or programmes on the bio-geophysical environment and on man’s health and well-being.

Solid State and Materials Analysis

Currently in Kenya we have advanced materials research in the field of materials attributed to growing demand for building materials, Road Construction materials, Engineering Materials, Manufacturing materials and Mineralogy. Our Laboratories have the capacity to perform.

We provide chemical and microbial analysis of drinking water, wastewater, air, soil, plants, and other environmental samples. Our services also include noise and light assessments, product formulation, pesticide residue testing, EIA/EA, OSH, and more.
